# FAQ

## How do I invite my team to a workspace?

Open **Members**, click **Invite**, enter the teammate's email, choose a role, and send the invite. Use **Administrator** only when the teammate needs workspace-level setup permissions.

## Why does the live site sometimes look unchanged?

Browser and Slack previews can cache favicons, social previews, and site chrome. Hard refresh the page and open it in a private window if you need to verify the latest published version.

## What should I test after a branding change?

Check the header logo, favicon, homepage cover, sidebar style, search bar, footer links, and dark or light mode behavior.
