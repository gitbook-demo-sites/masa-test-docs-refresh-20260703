# Invite your team

Admins can invite teammates from the workspace members page.

{% stepper %}
{% step %}
### Open workspace members

In GitBook, open the workspace, then go to **Members**.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Invite the teammate

Click **Invite**, enter the teammate's email address, and choose the right role.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Pick the access level

Use **Administrator** for people who need to manage branding, publishing, billing, or workspace access. Use a lower role for teammates who only need to write or review content.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Confirm access

Ask the teammate to refresh GitBook and confirm they can see the workspace and the target site.
{% endstep %}
{% endstepper %}

## Role guidance

| Role | Best for |
| --- | --- |
| Administrator | Workspace setup, site customization, publishing settings, and member access. |
| Creator | Creating and editing content. |
| Reviewer | Commenting, reviewing, and approving changes. |
| Reader | Viewing content without editing. |
